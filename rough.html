Who Are We

We're here to gather you together"- not only a motto, but a vision, an inspiration, a motive that came in the mind of some Cantonmentians of Chattogram and Dhaka. There are more than 80+ Cantonment institutions in all over Bangladesh. Each Cantonment has a community inside them. Whether it is an Alumni Association or Student Association, that are mainly built for the help of current & ex-students. But still, there was no Community which works for all the Cantonments together. We have also noticed that, there is no bonding or connection between the students of two Cantonments together. So, regarding all the issues we have get on the field to pursue all the visions to reality. Our first target, was to gather at least one student from each & every Cantonment of Bangladesh. Thus, from the starting to our 6th month we've already gathered more than 20000 students from many Cantonments across Bangladesh. Thus we've came with the motto, "We're here to gather you together"
